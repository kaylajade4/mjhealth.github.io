MJ's Health Corner

Providing you with the health information you and your family need

As a health consumer, you should have easy access to reliable health information that is easy to understand and interpret, to allow you to make the best health decisions for you and your family.

This website aims to provide you with an overview of the common conditions experienced in the community, as well as provide you with information about common medicines used to treat these conditions, as well as, appropriate advice.

All of the information on this website has been written by a local New Zealand pharmacist, ensuring that the information is relevant to the New Zealand community.
